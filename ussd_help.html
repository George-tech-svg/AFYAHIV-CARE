📱 AFYAHIV CARE - USSD Guide

Works on ANY mobile phone - no smartphone or internet required!

Dial: *384*1234#

📋 Main Menu (Registered Patients)

1 Report your health
Example: Type "Nina homa na kichwa" or "I have fever and headache"
2 Check medication reminder
Shows your medication time and regimen
3 Find nearest hospital
Get directions to the closest health facility
4 Change language
Switch between English and Kiswahili
0 Exit

🆕 New User Registration

Dial *384*1234# and follow prompts to register
You'll need: Full name, Location, Language preference

💡 How It Works

  1. Dial *384*1234# on any phone
  2. Follow the on-screen menu
  3. Type your response and press Send
  4. Your report is sent to your doctor instantly!
  5. AI analyzes your message for emergencies
  6. Doctor receives alert on their dashboard

🌍 Supported Languages

  • 🇬🇧 English
  • 🇰🇪 Kiswahili
